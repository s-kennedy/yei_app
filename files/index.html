Al Manar Career Counseling

The intervention is a career counseling initiative with the overall goal of providing strategic leadership in developing and sharing knowledge and expertise in career guidance policy, practice, information and initiatives towards improving and maintaining quality career guidance that will benefit youth and adult Jordanians.

"Prosper" Vocational Training and Apprenticeship Programme

The overall objective of the "Prosper" Vocational Training and Apprenticeship Programme is to increase the profitability of micro-enterprises by consolidating them at a local and regional level.

Agricultural development in El Hamma Djerid

The programme targets young rural women and men in the Sodad oasis, El Hamma Djerid, and provides skills training and entrepreneurship training in agriculture.

2GetThere

The 2GetThere programme combats youth unemployment through peer-to-peer coaching. The programme aims to prevent school drop-outs and connect young people to social services as necessary.

About YEI

The Youth Employment Inventory is the first comprehensive database to provide comparative information on youth employment interventions worldwide. Comprising more than 400 youth employment programs from around 90 countries, YEI documents program design, implementation, and achieved results.
